This section is all about the fundamentals of color theory.

Primary, Secondary, Tertiary Colors

Primary: Red, Yellow, Blue. These are the base colors.
Secondary: Orange, Green, Violet. Made by mixing two primaries.
Tertiary: Made by mixing a primary and a secondary color.

Warm vs. Cool Colors

Warm colors (reds, oranges, yellows) evoke warmth because they remind us of things like the sun or fire.
Cool colors (blues, greens, purples) evoke a feeling of calm or coldness, like water or the sky.
